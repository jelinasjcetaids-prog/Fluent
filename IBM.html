SCENE 6 OF 6
Strategic Recommendations for Growth

Data-driven recommendations derived from the full IBM Cognos analysis to drive revenue growth in FY 2025.

1. Dynamic Pricing for Shoulder Season
Implement 15–25% discounts for July–August bookings to lift occupancy from 58% to 70%.
2. Shift Booking Channel Mix to Direct
Direct bookings yield higher ratings (8.4) and lower commission costs. Target 40% direct by FY25.
3. F&B Upsell Programme for Delhi & Jaipur
Low-performing properties have F&B/room revenue ratios 40% below Goa. Targeted upselling training needed.
4. Service Recovery at Low-Rated Properties
3 properties below 6/10 need immediate service improvement programs to protect brand equity.
